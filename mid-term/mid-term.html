About Discount Electronics

Discount Electronics was founded in 1997 and is headquartered Austin, Texas. We employ over 100 people and operate 5 retail stores in Austin, San Antonio and Round Rock in addition to our online store. We specialize in computer products but carry a wide range of electronics from all brands and also provide electronics repair services in our Texas stores.
